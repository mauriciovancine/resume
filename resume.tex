\section{Summary}
I am an Ecologist with a PhD in Ecology from UNESP, Brazil. My expertise includes spatial ecology, ecological modeling, and species distribution modeling (SDM). I have published over 30 papers on biodiversity and climate change in South America. I also develop tools for landscape metrics in R and GRASS GIS, and have extensive experience teaching R.
